Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026.

Viskora builds tools for personal visibility and accountability. We aim to keep data handling clear and practical.

Viskora Spend

The current Viskora Spend app stores expense entries, budgets, categories, recurring expense templates, reminder settings, and theme settings in your browser storage. The app does not require an account in its current form.

Exports and backups

If you export CSV or JSON backup files, those files are created for your use. You control where those files are saved or shared.

Notifications

If reminders are enabled, the app may use browser notification permission to show reminders. Notification settings are stored locally.

Future services

If Viskora adds accounts, sync, payments, analytics, or hosted data features, this policy will be updated before those features are used in production.
