FAQ

Questions people may ask first.

What is Viskora?

Viskora is a product brand for simple tracking, visibility, and accountability tools. The first product is Viskora Spend.

What is Viskora Spend?

Viskora Spend is a monthly spend tracker for recording expenses, budgets, categories, recurring payments, and trends.

Is Viskora only for finance?

No. Finance is the first use case. The wider brand can support any personal tool where visibility and accountability matter.

Where is my data stored?

The current spend tracker stores data in your browser. You can export CSV files and JSON backups from the app.
