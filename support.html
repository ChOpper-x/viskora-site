Support

How can we help?

For Viskora Spend, most data is currently stored in your browser. If you change browsers, reinstall, or clear site storage, use Backup JSON first so you can restore your history later.

Common help

  • Use Export CSV for spreadsheet-friendly expense history.
  • Use Backup JSON when moving full app data, including categories, budgets, settings, and recurring expenses.
  • If reminders do not show, check browser notification permissions and keep the app open or revisit it near the reminder time.
